About

The story of PastPages

I cre­ated PastPages be­cause I think it ought to ex­ist. The shift­ing homepages of me­dia sites should be saved so they can be stud­ied.

I have no plans to make a profit. The site was launched using my own time and money, and it is supported by a network of individual donors. The code that powers it is open-source.

It is also a work in progress. In the near future, I hope to greatly expand the number of sites in the archive and make many other improvements.

Finally, I am the editor and the publisher. If you dislike something about the site, contact me.

— Ben Welsh
